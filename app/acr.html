Authorized Certified Representatives
ACRs – electrical subcontractors that are local to the project and trained according to autStand standards to use our specialized tools and online technology.
ACR Program key advantages
  • Flexibility in terms of project resources and project schedule, due to ACRs presence being local to the project site.
  • Reliability of ACRs, which are fully trained using our standard processes and tools with tasks delivered daily on demand. 
  • Less time needed on-site, due to real-time online project progress tracking for all stakeholders.
